2025-10-25

On July 18, 2024, I had the honor of representing Nanjing in the Jiangsu
Youth Rhythmic Gymnastics Championship, competing in the group event
with a five-person hoop routine. Our team worked tirelessly in
preparation, focusing on synchronization, precision, and artistic
expression. Every practice required patience, discipline, and constant
attention to detail, as even the slightest misalignment could affect the
overall performance.

During the competition, we faced strong teams from across the province.
Despite the pressure, our coordination and collective effort allowed us
to perform confidently and gracefully. Ultimately, our team achieved
second place in the group event, a result that reflected both our hard
work and the trust we built among teammates.

In addition to the team achievement, I was awarded the National Level 2
Athlete certification, marking a significant milestone in my personal
development as a rhythmic gymnast. This experience reinforced the
importance of perseverance, teamwork, and self-discipline. Participating
in this competition strengthened my ability to manage pressure,
communicate effectively with teammates, and pursue excellence with
dedication. It was an invaluable experience that deepened my passion for
rhythmic gymnastics and inspired me to continue striving for higher
goals in the sport.
